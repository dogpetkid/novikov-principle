Controls

  • WASD / Arrow keys: to move.
  • Space: "jump".
  • Zero (0): restart room.
  • ESC: Go to menu.
  • F4: Fullscreen (works only in game).

Objects

  • Player: the box you play as.
  • Lotus & Shadow: The Lotus shows the time till the Shadow spawns and the Shadow chases your exact path
  • Red button: pushed when you walk over it
  • Purple button: pushed when the Shadow walks over it
